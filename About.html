Gregory Colston, LEED AP, has been practicing architecture for over 18 years. His design principles are predicated on a few basic architectural ideas:

“Simple concepts, singular moves, and layered materiality project spatial comfort and clarity without cogitation.”

Greg and his team are committed to a hands-on experience and the highest level of customer support. Gregory Colston Architect has worked on several multimillion-dollar projects of varied architectural typologies.

Dedication to environmental responsibility is fundamental to Gregory Colston Architect’s philosophy. Constructing and operating buildings is resource intensive, and GCA strives to minimize this impact by designing energy-efficient buildings that incorporate sustainable technologies and renewable materials.

A growing public awareness of the importance of this mission — and the proliferation of products and techniques that enable responsible construction — is inspirational. Architects explore innovative and expressive ways to apply sustainable technologies and adapt tried solutions that complement the firm’s rigorous designs.

Highly collaborative in nature, the firm approaches design as a dialogue between client, design team, and site. Each project creates challenges and solutions relevant to the project environs and tailored to a client’s specific needs. Whether your project is an interior fit-up or a ground-up structure, Gregory Colston Architect has the experience to deliver a successful, innovative design.
